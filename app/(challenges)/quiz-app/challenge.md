Challenge: Quiz App
1 - Hard-code at least 3 questions, each with 4 answer options
2 - Show one question at a time with its answer choices as buttons
3 - Highlight the selected answer — green if correct, red if wrong
4 - Advance to the next question with a "Next" button (disabled until an answer is picked)
5 - After the last question, show a results screen with the final score
