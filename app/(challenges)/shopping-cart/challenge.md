Challenge: Shopping Cart

1 - Display a list of products (name + price) — hard-code at least 4
2 - Each product has an "Add to cart" button
3 - Show a cart section listing added items with their quantities
4 - Adding the same product twice increases its quantity, not adds a duplicate
5 - Show the total price of all items in the cart
6 - Add a "Remove" button per cart item that removes it entirely
